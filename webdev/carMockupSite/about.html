The 1990 BMW 325i 4-door sedan is a classic, blending performance and
practicality with a manual 5-speed transmission. Powered by a 170-horsepower
inline 6-cylinder engine, it delivers a satisfying rear-wheel-drive experience.
With a top speed of 196 km/h (122 mph) and an acceleration from 0 to 100 km/h
in 9 seconds, this car embodies the balanced performance expected from BMW’s
iconic 3 Series. On the road, it offers respectable fuel efficiency for its time
—8.5 L/100 km (28 MPG US) on the highway and 11.8 L/100 km (20 MPG US)
in the city. The 64-liter fuel tank ensures decent range, making it practical
for both city commutes and longer drives. The 325i’s combination of sporty
handling and everyday usability makes it a standout choice for driving enthusiasts
who appreciate the feel of a classic manual sedan.
