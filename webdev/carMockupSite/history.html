Learn More About The E30 Below!

The BMW E30, produced from 1982 to 1994, is one of the most iconic models in BMW's history. As the second generation of the 3 Series, the E30 was available in various body styles, including sedan, coupe, convertible, and touring. It became a symbol of driving precision and performance, especially with the introduction of the first M3 in 1986, which dominated motorsports. The E30 featured a range of inline-4 and inline-6 engines and was praised for its balanced handling, making it a favorite among enthusiasts. Its timeless design and engineering excellence have cemented the E30 as a classic in automotive culture.
